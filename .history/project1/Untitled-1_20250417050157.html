Tokyo

Welcome to Tokyo, Japan's bustling capital filled with neon lights and endless excitement.

Kyoto

Kyoto is home to temples, geisha culture, and stunning natural beauty like bamboo forests.

Osaka

Osaka is known for street food, friendly locals, and a fantastic modern vibe.

Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji is an iconic mountain and a must-see landmark of Japan. Great for hiking and photography.

Nara

Nara is a historic city known for its peaceful parks and friendly deer.

Hokkaido

Hokkaido is famous for its beautiful snow-covered landscapes, ideal for winter sports.

Okinawa

Okinawa offers beautiful beaches, clear waters, and a unique island culture.

Nagasaki

Learn about Japan's history and culture through Nagasaki's beautiful architecture and scenic views.

Sendai

Sendai is known for its green landscapes, historic buildings, and delicious food.

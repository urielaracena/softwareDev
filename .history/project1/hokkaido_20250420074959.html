Welcome to Hokkaido

Japan's northernmost island, Hokkaido is a paradise for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. With its vast national parks, volcanic mountains, crystal-clear lakes, and renowned hot springs, Hokkaido offers a refreshing escape from the country's bustling cities. Experience four distinct seasons, from lavender fields in summer to world-class powder snow in winter.

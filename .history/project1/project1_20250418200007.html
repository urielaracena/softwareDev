Rich Culture

Historic Temples of Kyoto

Experience centuries-old shrines and tranquil gardens in Kyoto, the heart of traditional Japanese culture.

Vibrant Festivals

From cherry blossom celebrations to lantern festivals, Japanese matsuri bring communities together through color and tradition.

Delicious Cuisine

Sushi & Seafood

Fresh from the ocean, sushi is an art form in Japan. Try it at a conveyor belt restaurant or a Michelin-starred spot.

  • Sashimi
  • Temaki
  • Uni
  • Toro

Ramen Varieties

Slurp your way through rich broths and springy noodles — each region of Japan has its own twist on this comfort food favorite.

  • Tonkotsu (pork bone)
  • Shoyu (soy sauce)
  • Miso
  • Shio (salt)

Breathtaking Nature

Mount Fuji

Japan’s iconic volcano stands tall and majestic. Hike its trails or admire the view from nearby lakes and towns.

Bamboo Forests

Step into another world as you wander through the whispering green stalks of Arashiyama in Kyoto.

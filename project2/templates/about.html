About Dianna’s Salon

A cozy beauty haven in the heart of New York City

Dianna’s Salon is a locally-owned beauty studio founded with passion and purpose. Nestled in a vibrant neighborhood of New York City, we’ve created a welcoming, intimate space where self-care meets artistry.

Since opening our doors, we’ve been dedicated to delivering personalized beauty services that celebrate individuality. Whether you're coming in for a fresh haircut, a color transformation, or a relaxing treatment, our small but talented team is here to help you look and feel your best.

At Dianna’s, we believe beauty shouldn't be intimidating — it should feel like home. Our clients are family, and every visit is a chance to unwind, connect, and leave a little more radiant than you arrived.

Thank you for supporting a small business with big dreams. We can’t wait to welcome you!

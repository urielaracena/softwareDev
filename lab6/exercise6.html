Plant Care

  • Orchids

    Orchids thrive with minimal watering and filtered light, making them ideal for beginners or low-maintenance setups.

    Easy Care
  • Roses

    Roses require regular pruning, sunlight, and pest monitoring, making them moderately demanding but manageable with attention.

    Medium Care
  • Gardenias

    Gardenias are sensitive to soil pH, humidity, and temperature changes, requiring close attention and consistent care.

    High Care
